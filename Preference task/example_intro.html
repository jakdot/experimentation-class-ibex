In this experiment, you will be asked to read a number of English sentences. Each sentence will be followed by 2 paraphrases. You will be asked to select the best paraphrase of the sentence. Only one paraphrase can be chosen. Thus, in case you find both paraphrases possible, you should choose the one that seems more appropriate.

You can select the paraphrase by clicking on it with your mouse.

In selecting the best paraphrase of a sentence, you should consider how you would understand the sentence if someone uttered it. It usually helps to imagine the situation that the sentence describes and then to decide which of the paraphrases better fits the imagined situation. There are no right or wrong answers here beyond your own intuition. You should answer each question as quickly as you can, but without rushing.
